FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a brand identity project take?+

Most brand identity packages are completed within 3–7 business days depending on the scope. The Professional and Enterprise packages may take 7–14 days. Rush delivery (48hrs) is available for an additional fee.

How many revisions do I get?+

The Starter package includes 2 revision rounds. The Professional and Enterprise packages include unlimited revisions — we keep refining until you're 100% satisfied with the result.

What file formats will I receive?+

You'll receive all the files you need: AI (Adobe Illustrator source), EPS, PDF (print-ready), PNG (transparent background), JPG, and SVG. For social media templates, we deliver editable Canva or PSD files.

Do you work with businesses outside Nigeria?+

Absolutely! We work with clients across Nigeria and internationally — including the UK, USA, Canada, and the rest of the African diaspora. All communication happens online and we accept international payments.

How do I get started or request a quote?+

Simply reach out via WhatsApp (+234 800 000 0000) or email (hello@praisegraphics.com). Describe your project and we'll get back to you within 24 hours with a custom quote and project timeline.
